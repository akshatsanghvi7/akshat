About Me

I'm Akshat Sanghvi, a final-year B.Com (Hons.) student at Nirma University, majoring in Accounting with a minor in Data Science. I’m focused on exploring how financial systems can be enhanced through technology, data, and smart analysis. My academic work includes a Corporate Governance Report and a BRSR analysis of the cement industry—highlighting my interest in sustainability, compliance, and data-backed decision-making. I’ve also worked with deep learning models like CNNs, GANs, and sequence networks, laying the groundwork for applying AI in financial contexts. Through my internship at Yash Jain & Company, I gained hands-on experience with GST filings, bank reconciliations, and invoice management using Tally—complementing my academic learning with real-world accounting exposure. As Head of the Cultural Committee and an NSS volunteer, I’ve built leadership experience and a collaborative mindset—both inside and outside the classroom. With growing skills in Python and financial tools, I’m continuously learning how to connect core commerce with the tools of tomorrow. I’m driven by one goal: to shape the future of finance through curiosity, technology, and impact.
